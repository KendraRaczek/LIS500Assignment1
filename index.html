Complications & Issues

Despite facing minor issues in the beginning with our interview, the process ended up going smoothly, and the extension for the assignment due date helped immensely. The first issue we faced was a member of the group dropping the class, therefore impacting the order of who would be partners. We solved this matter by reassigning partners once everyone entered the course and participated in the group discussion, which was also attributed to the extension for the assignment. The second issue faced was the time change between Central Standard time and Central European Standard time. Considering the seven hour difference, it was important to find a time that suited both of us to have our video interview fit within our schedules. Having the two extra days over the weekend helped us coordinate a time that worked best despite the time differences, which allowed us to upload our video before the deadline and gave our discussion group adequate time to make comments.

The Interview Process

Although recommended by the assignment instructions to use Google Hangouts with our UW-affiliated accounts to complete our interview, it ended up being very difficult to use this platform for the screen recording purpose. Instead of Google Hangouts, a member in our discussion group posted their video interview using a downloadable application called Zoom, which we ended up using instead to record our video. It was very easy to both use this application and save the file to be uploaded in our group later on.
